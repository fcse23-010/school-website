Frequently asked questions

What are the school hours?
Grades K–2: 8:30 a.m. – 2:45 p.m. | Grades 3–5: 8:15 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. Early drop-off starts at 7:30.
Does the school provide lunch?
Yes — hot lunch program (P580/year) with vegetarian, halal and allergy‑friendly options. Free/reduced price applications available.
Is bus transportation available?
Springfield elementary covers most areas within a 5‑mile radius. Routes are listed on the website. Contact transportation at 77523409.
When do school tours take place?
We offer tours every Tuesday at 9:30 a.m. during term time. Please sign up via our admissions office.
Do you offer sibling discounts?
Yes — 5% discount for the second child, 7% for the third. Combined with our financial aid program.
How do I apply for tuition assistance?
Request a confidential form from the finance office (kutlokedi@gmail.com). Applications due by April 15.
